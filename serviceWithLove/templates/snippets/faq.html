1. How much does your professional organizing service cost?
Our organizing services are billed at a fixed hourly rate. To book an organizing session, simply select our hourly service and include the item you would like to have organized in the notes section of the reservation.
2. Does your house feel cluttered? Unstructured? Are your cabinets full to the brim? Is it challenging to find the things you want?
With everything you have to deal with (career, family, personal life), home organization often falls by the wayside. That’s where we help, storing things you want to keep, identifying things you don’t, tackling clutter, and using your space more efficiently.
3. Is a cleaning service part of the organizing service?
Our team of angels offers the two services independently. If the client has their own staff to provide cleaning services, they are welcome to use them or schedule a cleaning session as well, solely based on your convenience.
4. What are the necessary tools to carry out the professional organization service?
Tools are understood to be the items necessary to carry out organizational and sorting tasks such as garbage bags, labeling machines, cable ties, sticky notes, boxes, baskets, filing systems, classification, and permanent storage and are the responsibility of the client.
5. Once the sessions are over, who takes care of the garbage or the things that are no longer required?
The client does the elimination of any garbage, donations or sale of any items that the client decides are no longer required. We advise them by giving information about companies that can assist with these tasks.
6. Who buys the organizational tools such as baskets, shelves, etc.?
Organizing tools, implements, or systems are purchased by the customer, but only after the pertinent evaluations and selections have been made. Buying these items in advance only creates more clutter and chaos prior to the organizing work. The purchase is made based on the recommendations of the professional organizing angels with the approval of the client regarding qualities, costs, styles, etc. The shopping activity can be carried out in conjunction with the organizer or on your own after the plan has been put in place and agreed upon.
7. Does the professional organization service require a single person or a team?
It depends entirely on the size of the project. In most domestic cases and some office cases, two of our organizing angels are required, but this is established in the initial evaluation visit.
8. What are the working hours for the professional organization service?
They are established around each client’s needs, as it depends on their own schedules. But these do include weekdays, weekends, mornings or afternoons.
9. Is the organizing work done by the Professional Organizer or the client?
The purpose of the Professional Organizer is to provide the necessary skills to the client, helping them to establish organization strategies and build proficiency in their organizing skills. That is why the ideal session has the Professional Organizer work together with the client. There may be cases in which either the Professional Organizer or the client works alone, but this will be established at the evaluation interview and/or throughout the organization project.
10. Apart from hiring the services of ORGANIZING WITH LOVE, should I invest in products for organizing?
The conditions in which the organization task is carried out must be adapted not only to the abilities of each client but also to their budget. In case there is a budget limitation to acquire organization products, reuse solutions will be found with the things that are available close at hand. These solutions will be established at the evaluation interview and/or throughout the organization project.
11. After finishing, do I receive additional advice?
Once the session is complete, the client will have the possibility of additional advice by telephone or by appointment during the first calendar month after the end of the initial session.
12. How long will it take to organize my space?
In the evaluation interviews, time estimates are established by space and/or project. These times can vary depending on the particular conditions. In the event that it is required to establish precise times of completion, they will be established and agreed upon with the client during the interview stage.
13. What guarantees does ORGANIZING WITH LOVE offer me?
As it is a service and not a product, the job guarantee refers to the level of customer satisfaction. To get the best results and the highest possible level of satisfaction, we suggest that the client work together with the professional organizer.
14. How do you know when it’s time to get organized?
  • You spend at least a few minutes every morning in mild panic because you can’t find something you need.
  • You need to rearrange the things in your kitchen so that you can get to the things you need. For example, you must move a device out of the way to use another device.
  • You need to shuffle a lot of outfits in your closet before coming up with something that really fits you.
  • You have thrown your shoes on the floor of your closet because you cannot deal with the pile.
  • You fold and fold, but your drawers always end up in a big mess.
  • You realize that most of your toiletries and cosmetics are rarely used
  • You just keep adding new mail to the existing stack every day. It is too overwhelming to face.
  • Piles of miscellaneous papers/children’s artwork/newspapers are the first thing you see when you walk into a room.
  • You can never find a pen/the scissors/the good knife when you want them.
  • Things are not kept in logical places and never go back to where they belong.
  • You cannot use a storage area for its intended purpose. Your car won’t fit in the garage, your desk drawer is so full of old bills that it can’t fit anything else inside.
  • The way you store things has caused something to be broken or damaged.
  • Flat surfaces such as countertops and tables are stacked with papers, books, clothing, and other sundries.
  • You have no idea what’s behind the cabinet.
  • You have more furniture dedicated to storage than dedicated to places to sit and relax.
  • Opening a closet, drawer or cabinet fills you with anxiety because things are likely to fall out.
  • You feel cramped in your home due to the amount of things that take up space.
  • When you get home at the end of the day, there is no place for your things to rest at night.
  • You have considered moving to have more space for your things.
  • You are discouraged by the appearance of your home.
